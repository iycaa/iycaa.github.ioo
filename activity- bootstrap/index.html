Royal Palace

A symbol of Madrid's regal past, the Royal Palace is the largest functioning royal palace in Europe. Its architecture and expansive gardens are stunning examples of European heritage.

Retiro Park

An oasis of peace in the middle of Madrid, Retiro Park is perfect for a relaxing day out. With beautiful sculptures, a lake, and vast green spaces, it's a favorite among both locals and tourists.

Beyond Madrid, Spain offers endless destinations waiting to be discovered. From the sunny beaches of Costa del Sol to the rich history of Granada and Seville, every corner of Spain is filled with wonders.
